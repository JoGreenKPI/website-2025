Meet Our Founder

After completing her degree in sustainability with a business major, Johanna engaged with companies to understand the barriers they experienced in their transition to sustainability.

This research, combined with her own experience as a sustainability consultant, revealed a critical gap: traditional sustainability management tools were either too complex for smaller organisations or too rigid for the dynamic nature of sustainability work.

"The day I met my 2-day-old nephew was the day I focused on being part of the solution. That change will be my report card to the baby who grew to be a man in a troubled world."

This personal commitment drives everything we do at GreenKPI. It's not just about building software—it's about creating tools that enable organisations to write their own chapters of environmental stewardship.
